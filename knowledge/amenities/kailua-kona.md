# Local Amenities — Kailua-Kona

## Grocery Stores

**KTA Super Stores** — Kona Coast Shopping Center, 74-5594 Palani Rd, Kailua-Kona, HI 96740. Popular local chain with wide selection of groceries, fresh produce, and household items.

**Safeway** — 75-971 Henry St, Kailua-Kona, HI 96740. Full grocery store with fresh produce, deli, and bakery. Open 24 hours.

**Island Naturals Market & Deli** — 74-5487 Kaiwi St, Kailua-Kona, HI 96740. Health food store with organic produce, natural groceries, and local products. Deli serves fresh made-to-order meals.

**Sack N Save** — 75-5595 Palani Rd, Kailua-Kona, HI 96740. Budget-friendly grocery store known for affordable prices and local products.

**Banyan Mart** — 76-6268 Ali'i Dr, Kailua-Kona, HI 96740. Convenience market across the street from the condo — snacks, drinks, and basic grocery items.

## Farmers' Markets

**Kona Farmers' Market** — 75-5767 Ali'i Dr, Kailua-Kona, HI 96740. Open Wednesday–Sunday. Fresh local produce, tropical flowers, Kona coffee, and handmade crafts.

**Kona Commons Farmers Market** — 74-5450 Makala Blvd, Kailua-Kona, HI 96740. Every Saturday. Fresh fruits, vegetables, local honey, and artisanal goods.

**Ali'i Gardens Marketplace** — 75-6129 Ali'i Dr, Kailua-Kona, HI 96740. Open Wednesday–Sunday. Open-air market with local produce, handmade crafts, art, and souvenirs.

## Big-Box Stores

**Target** — 74-5455 Makala Blvd, Kailua-Kona, HI 96740. Groceries, household items, electronics, and more in the Kona Commons shopping area.

**Walmart** — 75-1015 Henry St, Kailua-Kona, HI 96740. Groceries, clothing, electronics, household goods. Includes garden center and pharmacy.

**Costco Wholesale** — 73-5600 Maiau St, Kailua-Kona, HI 96740. Membership warehouse with bulk groceries, electronics, and home goods. Known for local products including Kona coffee and fresh poke.

## Drug Stores / Pharmacies

**Longs Drugs (CVS Pharmacy)** — 75-5595 Palani Rd, Kailua-Kona, HI 96740. Full-service pharmacy with prescriptions, OTC medications, health/beauty products, and everyday essentials.

**Walgreens** — 74-5457 Makala Blvd, Kailua-Kona, HI 96740. Prescriptions, health/wellness products, photo services, snacks, and household items.

## Medical Facilities

**Kona Community Hospital** — 79-1019 Haukapila St, Kailua-Kona, HI 96740. Primary hospital for the Kona area with emergency services, inpatient care, and medical specialties. ~10 minutes from Ali'i Drive.

**Kaloko Urgent Care** — 73-4856 Hane St, Kailua-Kona, HI 96740. Urgent care for minor emergencies, illnesses, and injuries.

**Urgent Care of Kona** — 74-5561 Kaiwi St, Kailua-Kona, HI 96740. Treatment for minor emergencies and illnesses.

**Aloha Kona Urgent Care** — 75-5607 Kuakini Hwy, Kailua-Kona, HI 96740. Urgent care with extended hours for non-life-threatening conditions.
