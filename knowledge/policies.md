# Policies & House Rules

## Check-In / Check-Out

- **Check-in time:** 3:00 PM
- **Check-out time:** 11:00 AM
- Late checkout may be available upon request, depending on the next guest's arrival. Please ask and we'll do our best to accommodate!

## Cancellation

- Cancellation policies follow the platform's standard policy (Airbnb or VRBO). Please refer to your booking confirmation for specific terms.

## Pets

- No pets allowed.

## Smoking

- No smoking anywhere on the property, including lanais and common areas.

## Noise & Quiet Hours

- Quiet hours: 9:00 PM to 8:00 AM
- Please be respectful of neighbors in the complex.

## Maximum Occupancy

- The number of guests should not exceed what was stated in the reservation.
- No parties or events.

## Parking

- One designated parking spot per unit.
- Please do not park in other residents' spots.

## Beach Equipment

- Beach chairs, towels, snorkel gear, boogie boards, and a cooler are provided for guest use.
- Please rinse all equipment after use and return it to the unit.

## Kitchen

- Please wash dishes and clean up after cooking.
- Do not leave food out overnight.

## Trash

- Take trash to the dumpster located in the parking area.
- Do not leave trash bags outside the unit door.

## Lockbox & Keys

- A lockbox code will be provided before your arrival for self check-in.
- Please make sure the door is locked when you leave the unit.

## Damages

- Please report any damages or maintenance issues promptly so we can address them.
