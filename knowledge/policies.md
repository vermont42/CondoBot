# Policies & House Rules

## Check-In / Check-Out

- **Check-in time:** 3:00 PM
- **Check-out time:** 11:00 AM
- Late checkout may be available upon request, depending on the next guest's arrival. Please ask and we'll do our best to accommodate!

## Cancellation

- Cancellation policies follow the platform's standard policy (Airbnb or VRBO). Please refer to your booking confirmation for specific terms.

## Age Requirement

- Must be 25+ years old to rent; proof of age may be required.

## Maximum Occupancy

- Maximum 5 guests. Exceeding the limit or falsifying occupancy information may result in eviction.
- No parties or events (grounds for immediate eviction).

## Pets

- No pets allowed. $600 pet fee plus damage costs if evidence of pets found.

## Smoking

- No smoking of any kind (including vaping and marijuana) inside the unit or on the lanai. $450 charge if evidence found.

## Noise & Quiet Hours

- Quiet hours: 9:00 PM to 8:00 AM
- Please be respectful of neighbors in the complex.

## Air Conditioning

- Keep AC set at 75°F or higher. The tropical environment means units struggle when set lower.

## Kitchen & Food

- Please wash dishes and clean up after cooking.
- Remove food and crumbs from counters, store food in fridge or airtight containers.
- Remove trash daily.
- Do not leave food out overnight.

## Trash

- Take trash to the dumpster located in the parking area.
- Do not leave trash bags outside the unit door.

## Beach Equipment & Towels

- Beach towels are provided for the beach — do not use bath towels outside.
- Return all beach towels; replacement fee charged if not returned.
- Rinse all equipment after use and return to the unit.

## Furniture & Unit Care

- Do not move furniture or electronics.
- Do not sit on furniture in wet swimsuits or with sunscreen/suntan oil.
- Do not spray sunscreen inside the unit or on the lanai.
- Wash all sand off before entering the building using the hose outside.
- Do not interchange items belonging to other units.

## Children

- Children under 12 must be supervised on the outdoor lanai at all times.

## Parking

- Park in assigned guest stall #6 or guest parking.
- Do not park in other residents' spots.

## Checkout Reminders

- Double-check all possessions at checkout. Mailing forgotten items incurs a minimum $75 charge.
- Check drawers, under beds, closets — remember charging cords, earbuds, kids' items.
- Log out of streaming accounts on all TVs.

## Damages & Liability

- Leave the unit as you found it. Charges apply for damage, missing items, or excessive cleaning.
- Report any damages or maintenance issues promptly so we can address them.
- Use at your own risk — owners not responsible for injuries or lost/stolen items.

## Illegal Substances

- No illegal substances (grounds for immediate eviction).

## Community Rules

- Guests must abide by Banyan Tree AOAO Pool Rules and Vacationers/Guests/Tenant Rules.
