# Banyan Tree 300

## Overview

The Banyan Tree, Unit 300 is a beautifully renovated 2-bedroom, 2-bathroom oceanfront corner unit located at 76-6268 Ali'i Drive #300A in Kailua-Kona, Hawaii, on famous Banyans Beach. The unit sits just 20 feet from the ocean with floor-to-ceiling windows and unobstructed panoramic ocean views. It features a wrap-around lanai where you can watch marine life — turtles, dolphins, whales, and the occasional Hawaiian Monk Seal. Banyans Beach is directly below for surfing and canoe racing, and there are tide pools to explore steps from the front door. You can also fish right from outside the unit.
