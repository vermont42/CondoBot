## Living Room TV

Smart TV with Xumu streambox. A single remote turns on both. There's a brief delay while it switches to the correct HDMI input. Navigate the Streambox menu with the remote. Sign into your own streaming accounts (Netflix, Hulu, etc.). Cable TV is available via the "Spectrum" app on the Xumu home screen.

## Bedroom TVs

Hisense Google smart TVs. Press Home for the main menu. Use directional arrows to navigate apps. Google Assistant button supports voice commands. You can cast content from your mobile device using the Cast icon. Sign in with your own credentials and log out before checkout.

## Amazon Alexa Speaker

Available for hands-free use: music, weather, timers, alarms, local info, and Bluetooth speaker pairing.

## Roller Shades

Chain-controlled. Pull front chain to raise, back chain to lower. Operate slowly and gently to avoid damage.

## Eero WiFi System

High-speed 1 gigabit WiFi. If WiFi has issues: check that Eero devices are plugged in (white light = online, red/blinking = disconnected). Restart by unplugging Eero for 30 seconds, then plug back in. If still not working, restart the modem the same way. On your device, forget the network and reconnect with the provided password.
