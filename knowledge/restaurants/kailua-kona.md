Nearby Restaurant Recommendations (Kailua-Kona)


Casual and Budget-Friendly

Da Poke Shack -- Renowned for its fresh poke bowls and casual atmosphere, Da Poke Shack is a favorite for a quick, delicious meal.

Basik Acai -- Enjoy refreshing and healthy acai bowls topped with fresh fruit, perfect for breakfast or a light snack.

Shaka Tacos -- A local favorite offering delicious and authentic tacos, burritos, and other Mexican dishes in a casual setting.

Umekes Fish Market Bar and Grill -- Known for its fresh poke and casual vibe, Umekes is a great spot to enjoy authentic Hawaiian flavors without breaking the bank.

Broke Da Mouth Grindz -- A local gem offering hearty Hawaiian and Filipino dishes at affordable prices, including their famous garlic furikake chicken.


Mid-Range

Huggo's on the Rocks -- Enjoy oceanfront dining with a laid-back atmosphere, live music, and fresh seafood.

Island Lava Java -- A popular spot for breakfast, lunch, or dinner, offering ocean views and a menu featuring locally-sourced ingredients.

Foster's Kitchen -- Known for its farm-to-table dining experience, Foster's Kitchen serves fresh, local ingredients with an emphasis on sustainability.

Humpy's Big Island Alehouse -- A lively alehouse with ocean views, offering a great selection of local craft beers and pub fare.

Jackie Rey's Ohana Grill -- A local favorite for its warm ambiance and a diverse menu featuring fresh seafood, steaks, and inventive cocktails.

Magic Sands Beach Grill -- Enjoy casual dining with a focus on fresh, local ingredients and beautiful beachside views.


Fine Dining

Huggo's -- For a more upscale experience, Huggo's offers fine dining with a focus on fresh, local seafood and stunning oceanfront views.

La Bourgogne -- A hidden gem for lovers of French cuisine, La Bourgogne offers an intimate fine dining experience with a menu of classic French dishes.

Ulu Ocean Grill and Sushi Lounge -- Located at the Four Seasons, Ulu Ocean Grill offers an exceptional dining experience with ocean views and a menu featuring sustainable seafood and local ingredients.

Papa Kona -- Offering a refined dining experience with a menu that emphasizes fresh, local seafood and stunning views of the coastline.


Family-Friendly

Pine Tree Cafe -- A casual, family-friendly cafe offering a wide range of local favorites, from plate lunches to burgers, perfect for all ages.

Quinn's Almost by the Sea -- A local favorite with a relaxed vibe, Quinn's offers a kid-friendly menu and a variety of seafood and American dishes.

Black Rock Pizza -- A family-friendly pizzeria offering a variety of delicious pizzas, calzones, and salads, perfect for a casual and satisfying meal.

Kona Brewing Company -- A fun spot for the whole family, Kona Brewing Company offers a range of tasty pizzas, burgers, and locally brewed beers in a relaxed, kid-friendly environment.

Poi Dog -- A family-friendly eatery known for its delicious Hawaiian and Asian-inspired dishes, including poke and teriyaki bowls, in a casual and welcoming atmosphere.


Shave Ice

Island Shave Ice -- A popular spot for refreshing shave ice with a variety of flavors and toppings.

Kona Shave Ice Company -- Known for its finely shaved ice and unique flavor combinations, Kona Shave Ice Company offers a variety of syrups and toppings.

Hawaiian Shave Ice -- Located in the Kona Coast Shopping Center, this spot offers classic Hawaiian shave ice with a range of fruity flavors.

Shaka Shave Ice -- A local favorite known for its delicious shave ice and friendly service. Offers a variety of tropical flavors and refreshing options.

Ululani's Hawaiian Shave Ice -- Part of a well-known chain, Ululani's offers premium shave ice with rich, natural flavors and high-quality syrups.


Kona Coffee Shops

Kona Coffee and Tea Company -- Known for its wide selection of Kona coffee blends and specialty drinks. A great place to enjoy a fresh cup of locally roasted coffee.

Holuakoa Cafe and Gardens -- Located a short drive from Kailua-Kona, this cafe offers Kona coffee and a selection of pastries in a charming garden setting.

Green Flash Coffee -- A local favorite for its strong Kona coffee and relaxed atmosphere. Offers a variety of coffee drinks and light breakfast options.

Kona Mountain Coffee -- Offers freshly brewed Kona coffee with a selection of light snacks and baked goods. A great spot for a quick coffee break.

Kona Coffee Roasting Co. -- Known for its freshly roasted Kona coffee and friendly service. Offers a variety of brewing methods and coffee blends.

Brewed Awakening Coffeehouse -- A cozy coffeehouse serving Kona coffee and a range of espresso drinks. Known for its relaxed vibe and delicious coffee.

Aloha Coffee -- Offers a selection of Kona coffee and specialty drinks. A convenient spot for a morning pick-me-up or an afternoon coffee break.
